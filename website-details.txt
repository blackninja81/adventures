# NAI Adventures - Brand Style Guide

## Primary Color Palette
- Deep Brown: #532D1D (Logo background)
- Golden Sand: #E5B168 (Mountain lines)
- Forest Green: #2C4A3B (Trees/Nature elements)
- Cream: #F5E6D3 (Background/Text contrast)
- Charcoal: #2A2A2A (Body text)

## Secondary/Accent Colors
- Sunset Orange: #FF7D55 (Call-to-action elements)
- Sky Blue: #A4C9E0 (Water/Sky elements)
- Sage Green: #9CAF88 (Secondary nature elements)

## Typography

### Primary Fonts
1. Headers/Logo Text: 
   - "Woodland" or "Adventure" for main titles
   - Fallback: "Trade Gothic Bold" or "Bebas Neue"

2. Body Text:
   - Primary: "Open Sans" or "Lato"
   - Weight variations: Regular (400), Medium (500), Bold (700)

### Font Pairings
- Headers: Trade Gothic Bold
- Subheaders: Lato Bold
- Body: Open Sans Regular
- Navigation: Lato Medium

## Design Elements

### Iconography Style
- Line weight: 2px
- Style: Simple, geometric with rounded corners
- Icons should follow the mountain/tree motif from logo
- Consistent 45Â° angles (matching mountain design)

### Photography Guidelines
- Natural, earthy tones
- High contrast outdoor scenes
- Adventure-focused imagery
- Landscape orientation preferred
- Minimal filtering, natural look

### UI Elements
1. Buttons:
   - Rounded corners (8px radius)
   - Solid fill or ghost style
   - Hover state: slight scale (1.05) and shadow

2. Cards:
   - Soft shadow: 0px 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.1)
   - Border radius: 12px
   - White background with accent color borders

3. Sections:
   - Maximum width: 1200px
   - Padding: 80px (desktop), 40px (mobile)
   - Background variations using primary palette

## Website Specific Considerations

### Layout Hierarchy
1. Hero Section:
   - Full-width landscape image
   - Centered logo overlay
   - Clear call-to-action button

2. Navigation:
   - Sticky header with transparent to solid transition
   - Mobile-friendly hamburger menu
   - Logo scales on scroll

3. Content Sections:
   - Grid-based layout
   - 2-3 columns on desktop
   - Single column on mobile

### Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: 320px - 767px
- Tablet: 768px - 1023px
- Desktop: 1024px+

### Animation Guidelines
- Subtle fade-ins on scroll
- Smooth transitions (0.3s duration)
- Parallax effects for hero images
- Hover animations for interactive elements

### Technical Requirements
- Optimize images for web (max 300KB for hero images)
- Use WebP format with JPEG fallback
- Implement lazy loading for images
- Ensure WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- Maintain 60fps performance

## Brand Voice

### Tone
- Adventurous but reliable
- Friendly and inviting
- Expert but not pretentious
- Nature-focused and sustainable

### Writing Style
- Active voice
- Short, clear sentences
- Rich in natural imagery
- Action-oriented calls to action

# NAI Adventures - Homepage Structure

## 1. Hero Section
- Full-screen background video/image slider
- Main headline and value proposition
- Primary CTA buttons
- Dynamic navigation

## 2. Featured Destinations (Popular Adventures)
- Grid layout with 3-4 featured destinations
- Large imagery for each destination
- Quick info: Location, Duration, Price Range
- Hover effects with quick view option
- "View All Destinations" CTA

## 3. Why Choose Us
- 3-4 key differentiators
- Icon-based design
- Brief, impactful copy
- Trust indicators (certifications, years in business, etc.)
- Social proof numbers (travelers served, destinations, etc.)

## 4. Adventure Categories
- Visual cards for different types:
  - Mountain Expeditions
  - Wildlife Safaris
  - Cultural Immersions
  - Desert Adventures
  - Tropical Escapes
- Category-specific imagery and icons
- Quick category description
- "Explore More" link for each

## 5. Featured Experience/Trip Spotlight
- Highlighted seasonal or popular trip
- Large imagery or video background
- Key trip details
- Special offer or unique selling point
- Direct booking CTA

## 6. Testimonials & Reviews
- Carousel/slider format
- Customer photos with testimonials
- Star ratings
- Trip type mentioned
- Integration with review platforms (TripAdvisor, etc.)

## 7. Instagram Feed Section
- Grid of recent Instagram posts
- Adventure-focused imagery
- Social proof through user-generated content
- "Follow Us" CTA
- Hashtag promotion

## 8. Travel Planning Tools

## 9. Latest Blog Posts

- "View All Posts" CTA

## 10. Newsletter Signup
